Halwa Puri Aur Cholay Ka Salan

Recipe by Chef Mah Rukh

Ingredients

Puri

  • Flour 2 cups (Makes about 20)
  • Oil 2 tsp
  • Oil for deep frying

Aalo Ki Bhujia

  • Medium size potato 1
  • Salt to taste
  • Chili powder 1/2 tsp
  • Haldi powder 1/4 tsp
  • Kalonji seeds 1/2 tsp

Cholay

  • Presoaked whole channa 1/2 cup
  • salt to taste
  • Zeera powder 1/2 tsp
  • Crushed Whole dries red chillies 1/2 tsp
  • Haldi powder 1/4 tsp

Halwa

  • Sujji (semolina) 1 cup
  • Oil 2 tsp
  • Sugar 1-1/2 cup
  • Water 1/2 cup
  • Seeds of small Illaichi 3
  • Yellow food color (optional)

Insturctions

Poori

  1. Make the dough with flour and 2 tsp oil and water as needed.
  2. Makeinto small parts (pancake like) as flat as you can and deep fry in hot
    oil.

Aalo Ki Bhujia

  1. Cut the potatoes into small cubes.
  2. Add spices and 1 cup of water and laeve on low heat.
  3. When soft, mash lightly.

Cholay

  1. Boil the channa with salt until soft.
  2. In a pot heat 1 tbsp of oil.
  3. Add spices and softened channa and 1/4 cup of water.
  4. Mix and smash slightly.
  5. Cook till bubbles appear then remove heat.

Halwa

  1. Put the sujji and oil in a pot along with Illaichi seeds and fry until slightly
    brown on very low heat.
  2. Meanwhile sugar and water to make a syrup.
  3. Add this to the sujji taking care not to burn yourself and cook on low heat
    to desired consistency.
  4. If you want to color the halwa add the color to the syrup before pouring it
    into the sujji.
